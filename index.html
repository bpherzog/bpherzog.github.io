About

Educational and Professional Background

  • Univ. of Massachusetts

    2012-2016

    I attended the University of Massachusetts Amherst for my undergraduate degree. I graduated cum laude with general studies honors with a B.Sc. in Mathematics with a concentration in Applied Mathematics and a minor in Computer Science.

  • Epic Systems Corp.

    2016-2018

    After graduating, I worked as and Integration Engineer on the Electronic Data Interchange team at Epic Systems in Verona, WI. While there I worked as a software developer with expertise in orders and results interfaces using HL7 V2 messaging standards, as well as an Interface Coordinator during an Epic instiallation project, and provided tier three techinical support.

  • Univ. of Maryland

    2018-Present

    I am currently a PhD student at the University of Maryland College Park in the Applied Mathematics & Statistics, and Scientific Computing program.
